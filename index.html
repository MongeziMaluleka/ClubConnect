About ClubConnect

ClubConnect is all about bringing WeThinkCode_ students together to explore and engage in their own interests through various groups and clubs. Whether you're passionate about technology, coding, or innovation, there's a community here for you.

Learn More

How to Join

Joining a group on ClubConnect is simple! Just find a club that interests you, and connect with the group on Slack. Stay updated with events, meetings, and activities, and start connecting with like-minded peers.

Explore Our Clubs

Robotics Club

Join the Robotics Club to explore the exciting world of AI, Robotics, and innovative technologies. Our members participate in hands-on projects and competitions, collaborate on groundbreaking ideas, and develop skills in programming and engineering. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's a place for you in our community.

Women Think Code Club

Empowering women in technology, the Women Think Code Club is dedicated to fostering a supportive environment where women can learn, grow, and succeed in tech fields. We offer workshops, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities that help our members gain confidence and build careers in the tech industry.

Other Activities

Tech Wednesday

Every Wednesday, we host Tech Wednesday where members come together to discuss the latest trends in technology, work on projects, and collaborate on innovative ideas. It's a great way to stay updated and connect with fellow tech enthusiasts.

Hackathons

We regularly organize hackathons that bring together students from various backgrounds to work on challenging problems, create innovative solutions, and showcase their skills. These events foster teamwork, creativity, and problem-solving abilities.

Halloween Event

Our Halloween event is a fun-filled evening where students can enjoy themed activities, games, and networking opportunities. It's a great way to celebrate the season and bond with fellow members in a festive atmosphere.
